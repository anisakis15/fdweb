CREATIVE DIRECTION PROJECTS

"666" Fashionfilm

"666" is a 3-minute long fashion film I directed, produced, and edited for my Audiovisual Communication final project.

The concept for this film was based on creating a blend of fashion and makeup and an eerie or even creepy aesthetic, inspired by demons and angels. I took care of the production by holding a casting for models, finding a stylist to help me come up with the outfits, contacting a videographer, and arranging the place and time of filming. I was also in charge of creative direction and the makeup. Lastly, I took care of the editing, making sure my vision came to life exactly as I had imagined it.

This project has marked a before and after in my work and sparked a passion in me for creative direction and production in the audiovisual world. Since then, I have also directed and produced a Videoclip, which has made me even more interested in working in this area.

During the filming, I also acted as a photographer to take the stills for both the poster and the thumbnail for the video, as well as behind the scenes pictures. For my final presentation, I designed this poster, overlaying two images I had taken, creating movement and achieving a scary yet intriguing effect. Finally, I designed the logo for Angelic Productions.
